# Overview

A small project of mine to study about OOP without relying much on AI (Copilot is only used for coloring and to make bug fixing easier)

## The features for now

- **Characters:**
  - **Kiko**: Mostly a support and healer
  - **Ampy**: All rounder in damaging capabilities

- **Combat:**
    - Just your usual turn based combat with skills and status effects

- **What to add next:**
    - Balancing basically (attack, defense, health, skill damage) everything cause the numbers are still all over the place (example is Ampy's Demonic Slash deals too much damage)
    - More characters to make the party to 4 members
    - Add more features to the enemy cause all it does is just attack s random party member

## How to Play
   - **(a) Basic Attack**: Perform a standard attack
   - **(b) Skill**: Use character-specific abilities
   - **(c) Defend**: Increase defense for the turn
   - **(d) Status Check**: View character stats and active effects
   - **(e) Enemy Check**: View enemy stats and status effects

Yeah thats basically it

## Character Abilities

### Kiko
- **Sharp Triangle**: Small damage attack
- **You can do it!**: Increases all allies' attack by 25% for 3 turns
- **Kiko's Blessing**: Heals all allies for 20% of their max health

### Ampy
- **Eternal Focus**: Next attack deals 2.5x damage (only works on himself.. or does it?)
- **Stare**: Reduces enemy defense by 25% for 3 turns
- **Demonic Slash**: High damage attack
